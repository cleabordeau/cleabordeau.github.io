Burger Shot:

The program

We realised this game on Khan Academy in Javascript using variables, expressions formed with arithmetic and logical operands and operators, conditional statements, loops, abstractions such a functions, arrays, oriented objects

How to use it



In this game you're a frycook at a new fast food joint. A line of customers is at the doors and hungry.
Click on an ingredient once to add it to the plate, once gain to remove it. Click on the meat to cook it, click on the meat on the hob when it's ready (green) to add it to the plate.
Be careful the meat can burn. Click on the plate to serve it within 15 seconds. If the client is satisfied by the food and by your time, they'll pay you 10$.
Remember to serve the burger when the timer turns green--any less and it'll come out raw and any more and it'll burn. Make 200$ to win!
